Monty Hall

A simple game I made in 2024, based on the Monty Hall problem.

This program recreates the famous Monty Hall probability problem. It uses dictionaries, loops, conditionals, and random number generation to simulate the game while allowing the player to test whether switching doors improves their chances of winning.
